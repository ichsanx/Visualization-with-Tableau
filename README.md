## Business Problem

Employee information is often stored in spreadsheets or operational reports, making it difficult to quickly understand workforce composition and organizational patterns.

The Tableau dashboard provides a centralized visual view to support workforce analysis and management reporting.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

- Summarize employee information
- Analyze workforce composition
- Compare departments or organizational units
- Monitor employee-related KPIs
- Support management reporting
- Present data through clear visualizations

---

## Business Questions

The dashboard is intended to help answer questions such as:

- How are employees distributed across departments?
- What is the demographic composition of the workforce?
- Which departments have the highest employee counts?
- How do employee characteristics differ across units?
- What workforce patterns require management attention?

---

## Data Preparation

Typical preparation activities for this dashboard include:

1. Checking missing values
2. Standardizing categorical values
3. Validating employee identifiers
4. Reviewing department labels
5. Correcting inconsistent formats
6. Preparing KPI aggregations
7. Connecting the cleaned data to Tableau
